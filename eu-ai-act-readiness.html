What Annex III Requires
Risk Management System
Continuous risk identification, analysis, and mitigation for your AI agent's lifecycle.
Art. 9
Automatic Logging
Your agents must produce traceable, auditable logs of every decision and action.
Art. 12
Human Oversight Measures
Humans must be able to understand, monitor, and override agent behavior.
Art. 14
Accuracy & Robustness
Documented performance levels, error rates, and resilience to adversarial inputs.
Art. 15
Data Governance
Training data quality, bias monitoring, and data lifecycle documentation.
Art. 10
Technical Documentation
Complete system description for regulatory conformity assessment.
Art. 11
Self-Assessment Checklist
  • Agents produce append-only audit logs of all actions
  • Governance traces map to regulatory controls (SOC2, GDPR, EU AI Act)
  • Kill switch / circuit breaker can halt agent execution
  • Delegation chains are signed and depth-limited
  • Human can review and override any agent decision
  • Agent identity is cryptographically verifiable
  • Compliance evidence bundles can be generated on demand
  • Risk management process is documented and versioned
  • Performance metrics and error rates are tracked
  • Data processing records exist per GDPR Art. 30
