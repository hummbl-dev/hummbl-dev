Self-Assessment Checklist
  • AI governance board or responsible individual designated
  • AI system inventory with risk classification completed
  • Stakeholder engagement process documented (affected communities)
  • AI risk assessment methodology established
  • Performance metrics and thresholds defined per system
  • Bias testing and fairness evaluation conducted
  • Transparency and explainability measures documented
  • Third-party AI/ML models assessed for risk
  • Incident response process covers AI-specific scenarios
  • Crosswalk to regulatory requirements (EU AI Act, state laws) completed
