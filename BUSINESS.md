## Sales Funnel

```
LinkedIn outreach / content / community post
    ↓ (10-20% response rate)
Free 30-min assessment call (cal.com/hummbl/30min)
    ↓ (20-25% close rate)
Readiness Audit ($5-15K)
    ↓ (30-40% expand)
Governance Implementation ($20-40K) or Retainer ($5-15K/mo)
    ↓ (referral)
Enterprise engagement ($30-60K)
```

### Pipeline Conversion Math

- 10 outreach/week = 2 responses = 1 assessment call every 2 weeks
- 1 assessment call every 2 weeks = ~2 per month
- 2 assessments/month × 20% close = 1 new client every 2.5 months
- Average first engagement: $10K
- 30% convert to retainer: $5K/mo ongoing

To hit $15K MRR by December: need 3 retainer clients. At 1 new client every 2.5 months with 30% retainer conversion, that requires starting outreach **now** and maintaining consistency for 9 months.
