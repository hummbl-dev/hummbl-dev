What SB 24-205 Requires
Algorithmic Discrimination Prevention
Use reasonable care to avoid discriminatory outcomes in AI systems.
§6-1-1703
Impact Assessment
Document AI system purpose, data used, evaluation methods, and mitigation measures.
§6-1-1702
Deployer Risk Management
Identify, document, and mitigate risks of algorithmic discrimination.
§6-1-1703
Developer Disclosure
Provide deployers with system capabilities, limitations, and intended uses.
§6-1-1704
Consumer Notice
Inform consumers when AI is making consequential decisions about them.
§6-1-1703
60-Day Cure
Remedy violations within 60 days of AG notice to avoid enforcement.
§6-1-1706
Self-Assessment Checklist
  • AI system inventory identifies all high-risk decision tools
  • Impact assessments completed for each high-risk system
  • Algorithmic discrimination testing conducted (disparate impact analysis)
  • Consumer notification process documented and deployed
  • Developer has provided capability documentation to deployers
  • Risk mitigation procedures documented and versioned
  • Internal governance policy assigns AI compliance responsibility
  • 60-day cure process documented with escalation paths
  • Vendor contracts include AI compliance obligations
  • Annual review cycle established for impact assessments
