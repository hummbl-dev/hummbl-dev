# Public Proof & Distribution Checklist

Use this checklist before posting or sharing public HUMMBL pages, case studies, conversion copy, newsletters, or social derivatives.

## 1) Surface Framing

- Confirm this artifact is on the right surface (public billboard vs internal ops).
- If internal reasoning is included, label it as `internal` or `internal draft`.
- Confirm legal and trademark statements are marked as positioning, not guarantees.
- Confirm any operational or roadmap claims are tagged as `historical`, `draft`, or `proposed`.

## 2) Evidence Posture

- For each factual claim, label one of:
  - `public evidence`
  - `primary-source synthesis`
  - `positioning / interpretation`
- Add an explicit date or snapshot anchor when using counts, tests, versions, or benchmarks.
- If a fact cannot be verified publicly in this repo, label it `requires verification` and avoid promotional framing.
- For legal/regulatory/compliance language, include `not legal advice` unless legal review has signed off.

## 3) Audience Safety

- Check the file is not presenting internal operator workflow as public product doctrine.
- Keep internal runbooks, destructive workflows, and draft governance as `internal` or demoted to `docs/plans/`.
- Remove or soften statements that sound like settled protocol if they are still in proposal form.

## 4) Claim Hygiene

- Flag absolute language: `guarantee`, `always`, `never`, `fully`, `100%`, `all`, `no exceptions`.
- Require caveats for any high-confidence but uncertain metric or performance statement.
- Ensure commercial assumptions are labeled `positioning` unless independently contract-tested.

## 5) Link and Navigation Health

- Verify all relative markdown/html links resolve.
- Verify badges/buttons point to working targets.
- Verify new files are discoverable from README or an intermediate README/doc index.

## 6) Governance Before Public Push

- Confirm no broken hierarchy: public claims do not outrank repo root instructions.
- Require at least one non-author review pass for:
  - legal/compliance framing
  - financial or commercial claims
  - dependency/security sensitivity changes
- Confirm no direct repo-canon claims are added without repo-local approval.

## 7) Closeout

- Post a concise status update in the packet/PR:
  - what changed
  - what remains draft
  - what still requires operator verification
