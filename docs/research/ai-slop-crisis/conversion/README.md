# Conversion Assets Evidence Posture

This folder contains **downstream conversion artifacts** derived from the research corpus.

- These files are designed to drive product thinking, marketing experiments, self-assessments, or sales conversations.
- They often turn research synthesis into defaults, formulas, thresholds, or persuasive copy.
- That makes them especially prone to accidental canon inflation.

Use these assets as **draft conversion surfaces**, not as self-authenticating evidence.

Before external reuse:

- recheck numeric defaults and benchmark figures
- recheck legal/regulatory/insurance claims against primary sources
- treat pricing, ROI, and risk-reduction assumptions as modeling choices, not facts

See [`../../../PROOF_AND_RESEARCH_EVIDENCE_POSTURE.md`](../../../PROOF_AND_RESEARCH_EVIDENCE_POSTURE.md).
