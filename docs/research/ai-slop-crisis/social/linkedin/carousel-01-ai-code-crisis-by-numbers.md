## Slide 2

**HEADLINE:** 42% of Code Is Now AI-Generated

**Body:** Nearly half the code shipping to production was written by a machine. The volume is here. The controls are not.

## Slide 3

**HEADLINE:** AI Code Has 2.74x More Vulnerabilities

**Body:** Across 100+ LLMs and 4 languages, AI-generated code contains nearly three times more security flaws than human-written code.

**Source:** Veracode 2025 GenAI Code Security Report

---

## Slide 4

**HEADLINE:** Security Pass Rate Stuck at 55%

**Body:** Despite two years of model improvements, AI code security pass rates have not moved. Better models are not producing safer code.

**Source:** Veracode Spring 2026 Update

---

## Slide 5

**HEADLINE:** 1 in 4 AI Samples Had Confirmed Vulns

**Body:** 25.1% of AI-generated code contained a confirmed vulnerability across 6 leading LLMs and 534 samples tested against OWASP Top 10.

**Source:** AppSec Santa 2026 (6 LLMs, 534 samples)

## Slide 6

**HEADLINE:** Devs Are 19% Slower With AI Tools

**Body:** In a randomized controlled trial, experienced developers took 19% longer with AI assistance -- while believing they were 20% faster. A 39-percentage-point perception gap.

**Source:** METR 2025 RCT (arxiv 2507.09089, N=16, 246 tasks)

**Body:** Three out of four enterprises are deploying agentic AI within two years. Only one in five has a mature governance model. That is a 54-point gap.
