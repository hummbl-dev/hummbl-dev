## Template 1: Cold Outreach — The Assessment Offer

**Who**: CTOs/engineering leads at companies building AI agents
**When**: First contact, no prior relationship

---

Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific thing — their company, a post they wrote, their product]. I've been building governance infrastructure for multi-agent AI systems — things like audit trails, delegation identity, and compliance mapping.

With the EU AI Act Annex III enforcement hitting August 2, I'm offering free 30-minute readiness assessments for teams running AI agents in regulated domains. No pitch, just a gap analysis based on working code I use daily across a 12-agent fleet.

Would that be useful for your team?

Reuben Bowlby
HUMMBL | hummbl.io

---

**Why it works**: Leads with their context, not yours. Offers value (free assessment) with a deadline (EU AI Act). Establishes credibility (12-agent fleet, working code) without bragging.

**When**: They've shown interest in the topic but don't know you directly

---

Hi [Name],

Saw your [comment on X / post about Y / talk at Z] — [specific thing that resonated or you can add to].

I run a governance stack across 12 Claude terminals daily — delegation tokens, append-only audit logs, compliance mapping to SOC2/GDPR/EU AI Act. The whole thing is stdlib-only Python, zero dependencies. Published the core as hummbl-governance on PyPI.

If your team is dealing with similar challenges around [agent oversight / compliance / audit trails], happy to share what's worked and what hasn't. No consulting pitch — genuinely interested in how others are approaching this.

Reuben

---

**Why it works**: References their specific content (shows you're not copy-pasting). Leads with what you've built, not what you sell. The "no consulting pitch" line paradoxically makes them more likely to ask about consulting.

## Template 3: Direct Ask — For People You've Already Talked To

**Who**: Existing connections, people you've met at events, community members you've interacted with
**When**: Converting a warm relationship into a business conversation

---

Hi [Name],

Quick question — are you thinking about EU AI Act compliance for [their product/company]? The Annex III deadline (August 2) is getting real and I'm seeing a lot of teams realize their agent systems don't have the governance traces regulators will expect.

I've been doing readiness audits for teams building with Claude/GPT agents — basically reviewing their architecture against the six Annex III requirements and telling them where the gaps are. Takes a week, costs [$5-15K depending on complexity].

If that's on your radar, I'd love to chat. If not, no worries — just wanted to flag it since I know you're building in [their domain].

Reuben

---

**Why it works**: Direct, specific, priced. No ambiguity about what you're offering. The "if not, no worries" gives them an easy out which reduces pressure and increases response rate.

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending ANY of these:

- [ ] Replace [Name] with their actual name
- [ ] Replace [specific thing] with something real from their profile/posts
- [ ] Replace [their product/company] with the actual company
- [ ] Replace [their domain] with what they actually build
- [ ] Check their LinkedIn activity — did they post recently? Reference it.
- [ ] If they work at a company with EU exposure, mention it specifically
- [ ] If they use Claude/Anthropic tools, mention the MCP server

**Never send the template verbatim. Always customize the first sentence.**
